My True Story and Experience

I've devoted 23 years to this field, discovering my love for style while flipping through magazines. In the 10th grade, I attended the College of Hairstyling in Kyiv, where I realized my passion. Earning two diplomas from the All-Ukrainian School of Style and Makeup, I then packed my bags and ventured to Canada, ready to take the next step in my career. This industry is not just my profession; it's a lifelong love affair, and I'm excited to share my expertise with you.
